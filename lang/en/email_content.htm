Please be advised that the e-mail addresses posted on this website are protected from unauthorized collection by e-mail collection programs or other technical devices, and violations of this policy are subject to criminal punishment under the Information and Communications Network Act.



Article 50-2 of the Information and Communication Network Act (Prohibition of unauthorized collection of e-mail addresses, etc.)

  • Anyone shall not collect e-mail addresses using programs or other technical devices that automatically collect e-mail addresses from the internet homepage without the prior consent of the operator or administrator of the internet homepage.
  • Anyone shall not sell or distribute e-mail addresses collected in violation of paragraph 1.
  • No one should use the email addresses for information transmission knowing that it is an e-mail address for which collection, sale and distribution is prohibited in accordance with paragraphs 1 and 2.


Article 65-2 (Penal Provisions)

Any person falling under any of the following subparagraphs shall be punished by a fine not exceeding 10 million won:

  • A person who has taken technical measures in violation of Article 50 (4).
  • A person who has transmitted the advertisement information for the purpose of profit in violation of Article 50 (6).
  • A person who has collected, sold or distributed e-mail addresses or used them for the purpose of transmitting information in violation of Article 50-2.
